How I Started Playing

When I was two or three years old, my mom inherited a piano from her aunt. My mom took piano lessons when she was little and decided she would teach me how to play.

[replace with video of my at 3 years old when I find video]

I learned some basic songs like Silent Night and Chopsticks. After a while I lost interest and My parents had gotten rid of the piano because we had to move. I didn’t play an instrument again until I was in the 4th grade and signed up for band class. I played the trombone until I was in the 7th grade and over those years I learned how to read sheet music.

I didn’t play again after that until I was 17, when my parent got their piano back, the same piano I had played on as a kid. I Liked playing so much that I bought my own digital piano for my room. This is also around the same time my brother in law got my into playing guitar. I have been playing instruments non stop ever since.
